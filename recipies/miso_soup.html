Miso Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso pastes
  • 1(8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onioins, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Steps:

  1. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat
  2. bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in tofu. Separate the layers of green onions.
  4. add them to the soup.
  5. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
